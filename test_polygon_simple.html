Coordinate Conversion Logic (Simplified)

The new logic uses a simpler approach:

  1. Screen to Sphere: Calculate relative offset from screen center (-1 to +1), then apply FOV-based conversion
  2. Sphere to Screen: Calculate offset from current view center, then apply inverse FOV conversion
  3. No complex ray casting or stage coordinate calculations
